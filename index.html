OpenBrackets


Our Mission

At OpenBrackets, we want to help bridge the digital divide. Inspired by the Black Lives Matter movement in Spring/Summer 2020, we wanted to make immediate change in our community. Through our course offerings, we provide support in computer science to middle and high school kids who may not receive it at home, and encourage kids all over the Bay to consider tech as a career. We also provide students with the opportunity to hear from Guest Speakers in the industry, to see the versatility a computer science foundation can provide.


Current Offerings

This fall we are offering 2 Game Design courses. For grades K-4, we have Game Design Juniors which will teach students computer science concepts through block-based language, Scratch. For grades 5-12, we have Python Game Design which will allow students to learn the computer programming language of Python through simple games that they create after each lesson. During these 5 week courses, students will learn how to tackle problems presented to them, the basics of Python, and will walk away with their own final game. You can see a more in-depth description of our lessons on the curriculum page.

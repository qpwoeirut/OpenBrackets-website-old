Our Mission

At OpenBrackets, we want to help bridge the digital divide. Inspired by the Black Lives Matter movement in Spring/Summer 2020, we wanted to make immediate change in our community. Through our course offerings, we provide support in computer science to middle and high school kids who may not receive it at home, and encourage kids all over the Bay to consider tech as a career. We also provide students with the opportunity to hear from Guest Speakers in the industry, to see the versatility a computer science foundation can provide.


Course Offerings

Last fall we are offered 2 Game Design courses. For grades K-4, we had Game Design Juniors which taught students computer science concepts through the block-based language Scratch. For grades 5-12, we had Python Game Design which taught students the computer programming language of Python through simple games that they created after each lesson. During these 5 week courses, students learned how to tackle problems presented to them, the basics of Python, and walked away with their own final game. You can see a more in-depth description of our lessons on the curriculum page.
